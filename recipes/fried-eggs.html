Fried Eggs

Description

Here we will be explaining how to properly fry some eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet to medium-hight heat
  2. Add butter and wait it stops sizzling
  3. Add eggs to the skillet
  4. Using fork, gently move the white off of yolk
  5. Wait for whites to be evenly cooked and serve the eggs
  6. Season with salt and pepper
