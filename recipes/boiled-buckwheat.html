Boiled Buckwheat

Description

Buckwheat is a highly nutritious whole grain that is considered a superfood. It is a good source of protein, fiber, and energy, and does not contain gluten, making it an excellent dietary alternative for people with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

Ingredients

  • buckwheat
  • water

Steps

  1. Take a cup of buckwheat
  2. Wash the buckwheat with cold water
  3. Pour buckwheat into a pot with boiling water
  4. Boil it for 30 minutes on low-to-medium heat
