Beef Steak

Description

We're going to explain the process of cooking a juicy steak

Ingredients

  • Beef Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat
  2. Season the steak with salt, pepper and garlic
  3. Brush the steak with butter
  4. Place the steak on the grill
  5. Cook for 5 minutes
  6. Flip the steak and cook for another 5 minutes
  7. Take the steak out of the grill and let it rest for a couple of minutes
