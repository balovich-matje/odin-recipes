Vegetable Salad

Description

A vegetable salad is a healthy and nutritious dish that can be made with a variety of vegetables. The basic ingredients include chopped raw vegetables such as cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, carrots, and bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • cucumbers
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • carrots
  • bell peppers

Steps

  1. Slice the vegetables into small pieces
  2. But them together in a bowl
  3. Add salt, pepper
  4. Mix the salad
